Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

TL;DR: BugWizard AI stores all your data locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or have access to your credentials, bug reports, or any personal data beyond basic analytics.

1. Introduction

BugWizard AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our web application.

We believe in radical transparency. BugWizard AI is designed to be a privacy-first application where your data remains under your control.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most SaaS applications, BugWizard AI does NOT collect or store:

  • Azure DevOps Personal Access Tokens (PAT) - Stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • JIRA API Tokens - Stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • AI Provider API Keys - Stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • Bug Reports & Content - Processed locally, never sent to our servers
  • Your Organization's Data - We have zero access to your ADO/JIRA instances

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information for basic functionality:

3.1 Analytics Data

We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand:

  • Page views and general usage patterns
  • Feature adoption (which features are used)
  • Error reports (no personal data included)

3.2 Waitlist Information

If you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address (for launch notifications only)

This is stored securely and used solely for product updates and launch announcements. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. How Your Data is Stored

BugWizard AI uses your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Application settings and preferences
  • Credentials (encrypted/hashed where applicable)
  • Bug report history (up to 50 reports)
  • Activity statistics

This data never leaves your browser. When you clear your browser data, this information is permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

BugWizard AI interfaces with third-party services on your behalf:

5.1 Azure DevOps & JIRA

API calls are made directly from your browser to Microsoft Azure DevOps or Atlassian JIRA servers. We do not proxy or intercept these requests.

5.2 AI Providers

When you use Ozzy AI or other AI features, requests are made directly from your browser to the AI provider's API (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, etc.). Your API keys are sent directly to these services, not through our servers.

6. Data Security

  • SHA-256 Hashing: Access codes are hashed before storage
  • No Server-Side Storage: We have no database of user credentials
  • HTTPS Only: All API communications use encrypted connections
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is in your browser - inspect localStorage anytime
  • Delete: Clear browser data to remove everything
  • Export: Use built-in export features for your bug reports
  • Portability: Your data is yours - take it anywhere

8. Cookies

BugWizard AI uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: For basic functionality (preferences, session)
  • Analytics Cookies: Optional, for usage analytics (can be declined)

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

BugWizard AI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes via the application or email (for waitlist subscribers).

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@bugwizard.ai (coming soon)
  • LinkedIn: Abdul Talib

Our Promise: We built BugWizard AI with privacy as a core principle. We will never sell your data, serve ads, or compromise your trust.
